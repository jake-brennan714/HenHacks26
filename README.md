# HenHacks26: Plant HP Bar
Made in 24 hours for the Henhacks 2026 Hackathon.
By Anthony Imperiale, Jake Brennan, and Daniel La Mastra.

Uses sensors to track the condition of a plant, displaying it as an HP bar.
Designed to make taking care of your plants fun and engaging.

# Technical Information
Designed for Arduino Uno.
